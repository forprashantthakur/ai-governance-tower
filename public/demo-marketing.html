Module 3 — Risk and Compliance Engine. Every model is automatically scored across five risk dimensions: data sensitivity, regulatory exposure, explainability, model complexity, and degree of human oversight. The weighted formula produces a risk score from zero to one hundred. Each model is then mapped to specific compliance controls across D-P-D-P, ISO 42001, and the EU AI Act — with pass, fail, or partial status and evidence attachments.
