AI Governance Control Tower

End-to-end platform for AI risk, compliance, data governance, and strategy — purpose-built for regulated industries

01 — Problem Statement
Why Does This Problem Exist?
Enterprises deploy dozens of AI models with no unified view of their risk exposure — each team manages its own models in isolation
Compliance with DPDP, ISO 42001, GDPR, and EU AI Act requires structured evidence — today this is tracked in spreadsheets and emails
No tool connects AI project execution (who is building what) with governance (is it safe to deploy?)
Data lineage, PII tracking, and consent for AI training data is manual and fragmented — a major regulatory liability
High-risk AI models (fraud detection, credit scoring, clinical AI) can be deployed to production with no formal sign-off process
When regulators ask "who approved this AI decision?" — most organisations cannot answer with evidence
AI agent behaviour (LLM prompts, tool calls, token spend) is invisible to risk and compliance teams
Organisations have no structured framework to prioritise which AI use cases to build first — decisions are intuition-driven, not scored across business impact, data readiness, and ROI
Translating governance requirements into automated workflows (Slack alerts, approval gates, audit trails) requires expert n8n knowledge most teams lack
02 — Vision of the Solution
One Platform for the Entire AI Governance Lifecycle
Single source of truth — every AI model, its risk score, compliance status, data lineage, and project history in one place
Governance built into delivery — compliance gates and ethics checklists are embedded inside AI project phases, not bolted on at the end
Risk-gated deployments — HIGH and CRITICAL risk models are automatically blocked from production until a Risk Officer formally approves
Regulator-ready at any time — audit trail, compliance evidence, and framework mapping are always current, not assembled before audits
Purpose-built for regulated industries — Banking, Insurance, Healthcare, Fintech — where AI decisions have legal consequences
AI-powered strategy layer — 5-step deep-discovery framework surfaces the single highest-priority AI use case, scored across 7 dimensions with ROI, n8n workflow, implementation roadmap, and PDF report — all AI-generated
No-code workflow automation — n8n Workflow Builder generates production-ready automation workflows from plain-English descriptions, instantly importable into n8n
Replaces 5–7 disconnected tools (Jira + Excel + email + MLflow + SharePoint + manual audit) with a single governed workflow
